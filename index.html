Day 1: Arrival in Nairobi & Transfer to Amboseli

You will arrive at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport and be welcomed by our representative. After breakfast, you’ll drive to Amboseli National Park where you’ll check into your luxury lodge with stunning views of Mount Kilimanjaro.

Accommodation: Amboseli Safari Lodge

Enjoy spacious tents with en-suite bathrooms, delicious local cuisine, and a pool overlooking the savannah.

Activity: Evening Game Drive

Spot elephants, buffalo, lions and stunning sunset views of the plains.

Day 2: Full Day in Amboseli

Wake up early for a sunrise game drive to witness wildlife at its most active. Return for lunch and spend the afternoon at leisure or enjoy a guided nature walk.
